About Us

The story of Sea Hawk Paints started back in 2001.

Mr. Hussain AlGabandi owned a Marine shop called Sammari Marine that had every type of equipment and accessories for all types of boats and yachts. Mr. Hussain realized many customers had issues with their boats having hull fouling, so Mt Hussain decided to seek for the best Anti Fouling marine paint and found Sea Hawk Paints in the United States of America, in the city of Florida. He made a deal to create and distribute the best and highest quality of marine paint in the Middle East, and that's how Sea Hawk Paints was created in Kuwait. Mr. Hussain couldn't stop at only creating marine paints, he decided to grow a factory into producing White Spirit, he saw that people had issues of getting the highest quality of it, so he built a system that produces over 200 tons a day of White Spirit and managed to get get the highest quality of raw material directly from KNPC. This is how Sea Hawk Paints was founded and managing to do the best in what it can and become the best in the Middle East.
